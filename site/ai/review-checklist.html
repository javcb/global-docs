AI review checklist

Before accepting AI-generated work, verify:

  • does it respect language boundaries?
  • does it avoid hardcoded credentials?
  • is the solution understandable and maintainable?
  • is reusable logic being placed in the right layer?
  • does it preserve repo/org boundaries?
  • is the SQL stored clearly where appropriate?
  • is this a prototype or production asset, and is that labeled clearly?
