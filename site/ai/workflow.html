AI workflow rules

Before coding

For non-trivial work:

  • inspect repo structure
  • identify likely files to change
  • propose a short plan
  • ask for approval before broad or risky edits

During coding

  • keep changes scoped
  • preserve current behavior unless asked to change behavior
  • prefer incremental extraction over wholesale rewrites
  • create notes/TODOs when something important should be revisited later

After coding

  • summarize what changed
  • list assumptions
  • list risks or untested areas
  • suggest the next best step
