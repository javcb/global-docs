Org: javcb-staging

Purpose

Work-in-progress, active development, refactors, and pre-production code.

What belongs here

  • Active project builds
  • Modularization work
  • ETL and automation in development
  • Repos being refactored from monorepo to modular

What does not belong here

  • Fully experimental throwaway code (use javcb-ai instead)
  • Retired projects (use javcb-archive)

Allowed languages

  • Python and SQL preferred
  • TypeScript/JavaScript/CSS allowed for front-end work

AI posture

  • AI tools may write and edit freely with human oversight
  • Maker workflow applies here (cursor/copilot builds, claude reviews)
  • Changes reviewed before promotion to prod

Promotion rules

  • Promote to javcb-prod after checker review confirms quality, durability, and rule compliance
